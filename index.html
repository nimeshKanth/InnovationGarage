IDEATE INCUBATE INNOVATE

We make the future today

The Centre for Innovation and Incubation, popularly known as Dr V.A.Sastry Innovation Centre (in the name of an alumnus of 1964 B.Tech batch who donated Rs 1 crore for this building) is a G+2 building, with a floor space of approx. 30,000 sft. The CII facilitates the following at present. Industries, who wish to establish their research labs, will be provided the necessary lab space depending upon their requirement. The industrialists / entrepreneurs can have access to use the other Institute facilities on payment of charges as per the norms of the Institute. The creative talent of students and the experience and expertise of the faculty are the key factors to motivate industries to open their R&D labs at the Centre for Innovation and Incubation of NITW.
